**Preview:** The funding option most SaaS founders do not know about

Hi {{first_name}},

Your funding options are not just equity or debt. There is a fourth way: Revenue-Based Financing.

Here is how it works in 60 seconds:
1. You receive a lump sum of capital
2. You repay a fixed percentage of monthly revenue (typically 3-8%)
3. Payments flex with your revenue - earn more, pay more; earn less, pay less
4. Total repayment is capped (usually 1.3x-2.5x the advance)
5. No equity dilution. No personal guarantees. No board seats.

The RBF market has grown 10x in 5 years. Providers are more sophisticated and founder-friendly than ever.
