## Email 2: Educational Value

**Subject:** Why NPS is not a customer health score
**Preview:** The behavioral signals that actually predict churn

Hi {{first_name}},

Here is a hot take that might surprise you: NPS is not a customer health score. It is a snapshot of how someone feels in a single moment. It does not tell you how often they log in, whether they have adopted key features, or if their champion just left the company.

A real Customer Health Score tracks behavioral signals:
- Product usage (login frequency, feature adoption, session depth)
- Support health (ticket volume, sentiment, escalation rate)
- Relationship strength (champion engagement, exec sponsorship)
- Payment history (on-time, failed charges, plan tier)
- Qualitative sentiment (NPS, CSAT, feedback)

Research shows behavioral signals are 5x more predictive than survey responses.
